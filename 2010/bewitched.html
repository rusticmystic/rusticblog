What to say?

Bewitched

A naive tuft of rose, in a gleamy morn clear
Secretly whispered to me, my heart did hear.
What to say dear?? I’ll lose my being, I fear.

A 'Maiden' sublime in a black cascade(apparel)
With a smile so sharp as a deadly blade,

From Valley of Beauty, she kisses the Moon,
Every glimpse of her face,enchants me to swoon.

Resides in my fantasies, that picture so clear.

What to say dear?? I’ll lose my being,I fear.

Who knew she wud so, disappear
Would be so far, and still so near

What to say dear??  I’ll lose my being , I fear.

Background

It was written in 2010 on insistence of a friend who wanted to gift a poem to someone.

Sun 14 February 2010

By Shivdeep
