Shikwa 

yoon umadh ghtaa,jab neelamber pe chhaa gyi,
guzrey zamaanon ki ek baat yaad aa gyi.

When the black clouds rose in the sky, that dark ambience made me remember an event form the past.

kaun thhey hum, kaun ho ab gaye hain,
kaunsi blaa hamari hasti mitaa gayi.

What I used to be and What I am now, What was that damn cause responsible for annihilation of my identity?

Ik arsey se thama rakhi yaad jo dil mein,
kyu ab humse yeh shairi krwaa gayi.

Since ages I had buried your thoughts deep in my heart, Why is it now that they erupt as poetry?

kyu sukoon si woh saawan ki baad-e-swa,
yoon ban aaj dasht ki zalim kzaa gyi.

Why that soothing cool breeze of the rainy season has transmuted into rash gush, as in the autumn in a dry desert?

jawani dhali bina machhli koi pakde,
pakeezgi k daawon mein buzurgi bharmaa gyi.

Because young age has passed without flirting with maidens, now this old age boasts of that piety.

chalo jo bhi hua sab quboola adab se,
par gaya na mann se shikwa yahi ki,

I acknowledge everything that happened in the past as 'good', ( I bow to Providence in gratitude), except one complaint, to the Providence, which still hounds my mind.

udte rhe sab parinde hawa mein,
bas koyal hamari hi pinjre ko bhaa gyi.

When all birds were free soaring high in the skies, why was that only my beloved nightingale chosen for the 'cage'?

Fri 20 July 2012

By Shivdeep
